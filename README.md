### Hi there 👋

I'm an Economist and Postdoctoral Fellow at Universidad de los Andes, Colombia, where I also serve as an Adjunct Professor. I hold a PhD in Economics.

My research lies at the intersection of Environmental and Resource Economics, Political Economy of Natural Resources, Conservation Policy, and Applied Econometrics. I study environmental governance, fisheries, forests, and common-pool resources, with a particular focus on developing countries and contexts characterized by institutional constraints and natural resource dependence.

Methodologically, my work combines causal inference, remote sensing, geospatial analysis, and increasingly machine learning tools to evaluate the socioeconomic and environmental impacts of public policies.

- 🔭 I’m currently working on environmental policy, illegal resource extraction, and deforestation
- 🌱 I’m expanding my work on geospatial data and machine learning applications in economics
- 👯 I’m open to academic collaborations in environmental and development economics
