### Hi there 👋

I'm an Economist and Postdoctoral Fellow at Universidad de los Andes, Colombia, where I also serve as an Adjunct Professor. I hold a PhD in Economics.

My research lies at the intersection of Environmental and Resource Economics, Development Economics, and Applied Econometrics. I focus on topics such as illegal fishing, deforestation, environmental governance, and the economic impacts of conservation policies, often using quasi-experimental methods and novel data sources (e.g., satellite data and geospatial analytics).

I am particularly interested in policy-relevant research in contexts of weak institutions and natural resource dependence.
